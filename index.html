About me

I am a Post-doc researcher at Liao Lab at the Whitney Laboratory for Marine Bioscience, University of Florida (UF). My post-doc mentor is Prof. James Liao. Now I am mainly working on fish biomechanics and its hydrodynamic mechanisms.
