About me

I am a PhD student in the School of Water Resources and Hydropower Engineering and I am also affiliated with the State Key Laboratory of Water Resources Engineering and Management. My supervisors are Prof. Yuhong Zeng. My research interests lie in swimming behavior of fish, fish passage facilities, vegetated flows, and sediment transport.
